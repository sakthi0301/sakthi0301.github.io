Simple Sponge Cake


Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 100g caster suger
  • 85g self-raising flour


Notes on Ingredients

Castor Sugar
Finely granulated white suger
Self-raising Flour
A pre-mixed combition of flour and leavening agents (usually salt and baking powder).


Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 190oC.
  2. Greare a 20cm even round cake pan.
  3. In a medium bowl,whip together the eggs and castor sugar until fluffy.
  4. Fold in flour.
  5. Pour mixture into the prepared pan.
  6. Bake 20 minites in the preheated oven until the top of the cake springs back when lightly pressed.
  7. Cool in the pan over a wire rack.
